Past Life Regression

Past Life Regression is a component of the QHHT® session where an individual is brought and guided through an appropriate past life. The individual’s Subconscious selects what past life to show while the QHHT® Practitioner guides them through it. Past life is related to a concept of reincarnation which existed also in early Christian writings. The selected past life is always in some way relevant to the current life otherwise we wouldn’t see it. It is quite common for multiple past lives to be shown during a single session. The Practitioner navigates through a past life using a series of questions to help understand the time period, social setting of the life, main life themes etc.

What Happens in the QHHT® Session?

Fascinating transformations happen during the session, both in the individual hypnotised and the surrounding environment.
